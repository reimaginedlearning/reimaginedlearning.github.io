Our Mission

We want to create regular opportunities for intergenerational learning, through the medium of reading by re-engaging an ageing population with school-aged children to improve learning outcomes.

Our Vision

We want to enrich the lives of the ageing population by reinvigorating and reimagining their role as tribal elders and fostering intergenerational learning by combining the accumulated wisdom of the elders and modern learning techniques. At the same time, the younger generation will be able to learn about the history the came before them and appreciate the impact it has on their lives today.
